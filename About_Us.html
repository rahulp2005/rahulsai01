About US

Who We Are

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) is India's premier stock exchange and a cornerstone of the nation's financial infrastructure. Established in 1992, NSE was created to bring transparency, efficiency, and innovation to Indian financial markets. Today, it is recognized globally as one of the largest exchanges by trading volume and market capitalization.

Our Mission

To drive economic growth by providing a platform for fair, transparent, and efficient trading, while fostering trust and inclusivity in financial markets.

Our Vision

To be a globally preferred stock exchange, contributing to India's economic leadership through innovation, technology, and customer-centricity.
